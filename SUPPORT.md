# Support ComfyUI Universal FP8 / NVFP4 Quantizer

[English](SUPPORT.md) | [简体中文](SUPPORT.zh-CN.md) | [한국어](SUPPORT.ko.md)

This project is free, open-source software. Sponsorship is optional and never
required to install, run, or update the node.

## About the maintainer

I am a South Korea-based, self-taught independent developer. I maintain this
project without institutional or corporate backing. Sponsorship provides time
for testing, compatibility work, documentation, and maintenance.

## Sponsor development

Support development through [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/thepororo).
Funding is used for ComfyUI and comfy-kitchen compatibility testing, large-model
validation, architecture support, documentation, and bug fixes.

Sponsors do not receive a functionally restricted build. Development previews
or roadmap notes may be shared early when practical.

## Bugs, requests, and paid work

Use [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/thepororo/ComfyUI-NVFP4-Quantizer/issues)
for reproducible bugs and generally useful requests. Do not post private models,
credentials, API keys, or personal information.

Installation help, model-specific tuning, and custom architecture support are
separate from sponsorship. Scope, availability, price, and delivery terms must
be agreed before paid work begins. Sponsorship does not guarantee support.
